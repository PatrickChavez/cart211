Chapter 1

It’s been several weeks since the creature appeared. News stations called it a “Mandrake” due to its plant-like appearance. Anybody who tries to approach it becomes gravely ill. Many died within the first days of its appearance. The entire city is in quarantine until the threat has been taken care of. So far, there hasn’t been much progress on the situation.

I’ve been exchanging letters with Eric during all this chaos. They’re nice to look forward to and help to distract me from all the problems going on right now.

I should probably look for something to do before I get anxious, maybe playing a game will help…

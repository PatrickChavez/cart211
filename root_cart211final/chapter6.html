Chapter 6

I’ve been alone at home for months now.

I’ve honestly lost track of time, days just blend into one another.

I feel like corpse, slowly rotting away.

Aside from going out to buy necessities, I haven’t spoken to anyone in a long time.

Communication between me and Eric has diminished to the point where letters are sent weeks apart.

I can’t just tell him I’m upset when everyone is feeling the same, that would be selfish.

I just don’t have the energy to do anything.

I barely eat, I barely leave my bed, I barely write. My body feels heavy all the time.

I feel nothing. I am nothing.

I just want to disappear.

I really wish I could stop thinking negatively, but it’s almost like a comfort zone to me.

I’m tired of thinking that this situation is going to get better.

I want to close my eyes and never wake up.

The End...
